Berry Good Lunch Salad

This delicious salad will leave your co-workers jealous, and it could not be easier to assemble in a hurry. The total prep time is ten minutes and there is no cook time!
It is very healhy, with 62% of your daily fiber, as well as 80% of the recommended daily value of Iron, and 40% of the recommended daily value of potassium!
This salad has 574 calories, so it is a balanced way to enjoy your lunch.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Organic Spring Mis
  • 1/2 cup Blackberries
  • 1/2 cup Raspberries
  • 1/2 cup Blueberries
  • 1 cup Seedless Grapes
  • 1/2 cup crushed Walnuts
  • 1 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar

Directions

Rinse all of the berries and grapes
Add the spring mix to a bowl
Top with the berries and Grapes
Use the Balsamic Vinegar as a dressing
Note: If the Balsamic Vinegar is too acidic for your tastes, substitute with a vinegarette.
Top with the crushed walnuts
Enjoy!
