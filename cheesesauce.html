Vegan Cheese Sauce

A very easy and simple vegan cheese sauce that is ready in under 5 minutes! Perfect for savory recipes, this is one of our favorites!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup canned coconut milk
  • 6 Tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 Tbsp tapioca flour
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Pinch of smoked paprika

Directions

Add all of the ingredients into a saucepan an stir with a whisk.
Once everything is combined, turn on the heat and bring the mixture to a boil while constantly stirring.
Let simmer on low for one minute.
Serve and enjoy!
Store leftovers in the refrigerator up to 2 days. Reheat with a splash of dairy-free milk.
